# Membership Plans

CodeMentor AI mentorship operates on a subscription model.

---

## 💳 Subscription Benefits

All premium members get:

- Access to Premium Telegram Community
- Weekly Google Meet Live Sessions
- Direct doubt support
- Career guidance
- Resume review
- Interview prep sessions

---

## 📦 Plans Overview

### Basic Plan

- Telegram group access
- Weekly group Google Meet
- Resource sharing

### Pro Plan

- Everything in Basic
- 1:1 monthly session
- Resume review
- Mock interview

### Elite Plan

- Everything in Pro
- Bi-weekly 1:1 sessions
- Priority doubt support
- Referral guidance
- Personalized roadmap

---

## 🔐 Access Control

After payment:

- User role upgraded
- Telegram group invite link unlocked
- Session booking enabled
